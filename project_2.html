HOMEPROJECT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM




This project is developed basically for institution's use whereby a supervisor will register a student under his/her supervision, then the student can chat with the supervisor, and even send write-up to the supervisor. The supervisor will download it, read and make the necessary corrections, the send it back to the student to correct it. After the completion of the project, both write-up and software, the student willthen commit it for every other lecturer to download and go through. More screenshots are below
